Rules

A Good MixRules

1. General Conversations
Insults and slurs are prohibited
Do not harass or use the threat of violence
No discrimination based on race, gender, sexual orientation etc
All members are required to have mutual respect in discussions or debates

2. Voice Chat
Irritating or annoying noises are prohibited.
No talking over or attempting to silence other members
No NSFW or Songs with Swearing in them.

3. Content
No swastikas/nazi propaganda
No KKK propaganda/images
No gore
No sexual images or verbal depictions
No NSFW
No advertising without permission from staff
All content must go in the correct channel

4. Spam
No repeating the same message and posting it in the same or different channels
Do not contentiously ping members, staff, or roles

5. Accounts
No offensive names or nicknames
No inappropriate user profiles
No usage of alternate accounts
Do not impersonate staff, Discord personnel, or other members

6. Discord ToS and Guidelines
The Discord ToS must be followed at all times
The Discord Community Guidelines must be followed at all times
